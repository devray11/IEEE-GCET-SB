About Us

IEEE GCET Student Branch

IEEE GCET Student Branch is a vibrant and pioneering community at G H Patel College of Engineering & Technology, dedicated to advancing technology, fostering innovation, and nurturing professional growth. As part of the globally recognized IEEE—with its unparalleled benefits such as exclusive access to the IEEE Xplore Digital Library, technical workshops, webinars, online courses, and a robust network of experts—our branch provides an invaluable platform for students to enhance their technical prowess and career opportunities.

Our Mission

At IEEE GCET Student Branch, our mission is to empower students with cutting-edge knowledge and skills, fostering an environment that encourages innovation and leadership. We actively organize and participate in technical symposiums, hands-on workshops, seminars, and student-led events such as the highly anticipated Student Professional Awareness Conferences. Our regular initiatives—ranging from "Techronicle," our branch's in-house magazine that keeps members informed about the latest technological trends, to collaborative events with other IEEE branches and chapters—ensure that our members are not only consumers of knowledge but also contributors to the evolving landscape of engineering and technology.

Our Community

With over 200 active members, and today, our branch is proudly guided by the leadership of Dr. Rakesh Patel as the Branch Counsellor and Jamila Kapadia as the Chairperson, alongside a dedicated team of energetic volunteers. Their unwavering commitment ensures that our activities are innovative, inclusive, and aligned with IEEE's global mission of advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. The branch continuously evolves, leveraging the extensive resources of IEEE to provide workshops in emerging fields like artificial intelligence, robotics, IoT, and cybersecurity, while also facilitating networking opportunities that connect students with industry experts and researchers worldwide.

Our Inception and Legacy

Our journey began when the GCET IEEE branch was approved by IEEE Headquarters in New York on February 28, 2006. The founding team, comprising 29 enthusiastic student members and 2 executive members, was spearheaded by Dr. Ketan Kotecha (Branch Counsellor), Mr. Nilesh Modi (Branch Mentor), and Prerit Souda as the inaugural Branch Chairperson. As the first IEEE student branch established within the Gujarat Section of IEEE's Asia-Pacific Region, our inception marked a significant milestone in making IEEE's world-class resources and professional development opportunities accessible to budding engineers in Gujarat. Over the years, we have built upon this strong foundation, growing both in membership and in the scope of our technical and professional initiatives.

Looking Ahead

As we honor our rich history and the pioneering spirit of our founding members, IEEE GCET Student Branch remains steadfast in its commitment to professional excellence and technological innovation. We invite all aspiring engineers to join us in our journey of learning, collaboration, and growth, and to take advantage of the myriad opportunities that IEEE membership offers. Together, we continue to shape a future where technology and humanity advance hand in hand.
